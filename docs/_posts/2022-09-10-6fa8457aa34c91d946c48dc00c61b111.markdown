Aspect category detection (ACD) aims to identify the aspect categories from reviewers expressed opinions in a given sentence, where one or multiple predefined aspect categories are mentioned explicitly or implicitly. With the boom of the pretrained language model, related studies have achieved significant improvements in ACD.
