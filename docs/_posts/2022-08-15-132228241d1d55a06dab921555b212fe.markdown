How can we detect and analyze hyperlink-driven misbehavior in online forums? Online forums contain enormous amounts of user-generated contents, with threads and comments frequently supplemented by hyperlinks. These hyperlinks are often posted with malicious intention and we refer to this as hyperlink-driven misbehavior. We present HyperMan, a systematic suite of capabilities, to detect and analyze hyperlink-driven misbehavior in online forums.
