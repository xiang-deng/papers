Non-factoid question answering (NFQA) is a challenging and underresearched task that requires constructing long-form answers, such as explanations or opinions, to open-ended non-factoid questionsNFQs. There is still little understanding of the categories of NFQs that people tend to ask, what form of answers they expect to see in return, and what the key research challenges of each category are.
