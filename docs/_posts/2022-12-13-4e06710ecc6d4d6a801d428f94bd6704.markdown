The rise of the Semantic Web opens new opportunities for qualitative reasoning research. It provides massive amounts of factual data that complement the ability of qualitative representations to express general principles and causal laws. This paper summarizes how we are bringing together three kinds of resources that represent quantities. The first is the traditional QR notion of quantities as fluents.
