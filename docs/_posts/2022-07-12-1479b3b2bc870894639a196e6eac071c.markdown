ClueWeb22, the newest iteration of the ClueWeb line of datasets, is the result of more than a year of collaboration between industry and academia. Its design is influenced by the research needs of the academic community and the real-world needs of large-scale industry systems. Compared with earlier ClueWeb datasets, the ClueWeb22 corpus is larger, more varied, and has higher-quality documents.
