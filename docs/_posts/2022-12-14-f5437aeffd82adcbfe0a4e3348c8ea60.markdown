A news article s online audience provides useful insights about the article s identity. However, fake news classifiers using such information risk relying on profiling. In response to the rising demand for ethical AI, we present a profiling-avoiding algorithm that leverages Twitter users during model optimisation while excluding them when an article s veracity is evaluated.
