With 1.3 billion users, Instagram (IG) has also become a business tool. IG influencer marketing, expected to generate $33.25 billion in 2022, encourages companies and influencers to create trending content. Various methods have been proposed for predicting a post s popularity, ie, how much engagement (eg, Likes) it will generate.
