Relationship tagging of long text documents is a growing need in information science, spurred by the emergence of multi-million book bibliographic digital libraries. Large digital libraries offer an unprecedented glimpse into cultural history through their collections, but the combination of collection scale and document length complicates their study, given that prior work on large corpora has dealt primarily with much shorter texts.
