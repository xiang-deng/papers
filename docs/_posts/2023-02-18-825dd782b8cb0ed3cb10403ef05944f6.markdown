Category tag of Point Of Interest (POI) serves a crucial role in numerous Location Based Services (LBS) like finding a place of interest, POI recommendation, location-based advertisement, etc. However, it has been found that many POIs (around 30%) do not have any category information. Manual tagging of these categories is a very costly affair as it requires a physical visit to each POI. Hence, an automated method for category tagging of POIs has been identified as an important research problem …
