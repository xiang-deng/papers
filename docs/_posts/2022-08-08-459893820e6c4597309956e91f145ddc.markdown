Named entity recognition is a technique for extracting named entities from text and classifying them into various entity types. There has been a lot of research done on the Punjabi language s Shahmukhi script, with less emphasis on the Gurmukhi script. This paper proposes a novel technique for extracting named entities from sentences written in the Punjabi language s Gurmukhi script, which categorizes the entities into six different entity types. 15 k sentences from the Indic data corpus Punjabi data and Cites: Deep contextualized word representations
