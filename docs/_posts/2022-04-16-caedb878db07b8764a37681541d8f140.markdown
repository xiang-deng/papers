Fact verification (FV) is a challenging task which aims to verify a claim using multiple evidential sentences from trustworthy corpora, eg, Wikipedia. Most existing approaches follow a three-step pipeline framework, including document retrieval, sentence retrieval and claim verification. High-quality evidences provided by the first two steps are the foundation of the effective reasoning in the last step.
