As the pandemic progresses and healthcare systems continue to adapt, it is important to understand how these changes in care have changed patient care. This study aims to use community detection techniques to identify and facilitate analysis of themes in patient safety event (PSE) reports to better understand COVID-19 pandemic s impact on patient safety.
