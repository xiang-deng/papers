We present an efficient and accurate long-form question answering platform, dubbed iLFQA (ie, short for intelligent Long-Form Question Answering). iLFQA was created as a continuation of iTA (ie, short for intelligent Teaching Assistant). iTA was originally designed as a narrow domain question answering platform that performed generative question answering with a single textbook as a reference.
