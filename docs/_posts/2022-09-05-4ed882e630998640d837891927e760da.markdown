In this position paper we argue that a conception of linguistic competence and conversational abilities that would fulfil the aims of Artificial General Intelligence cannot remain characterised as a static system of patterns induced from disembodied textual data. Instead, it should be modelled as a continuous, active, and interactive learning process.
