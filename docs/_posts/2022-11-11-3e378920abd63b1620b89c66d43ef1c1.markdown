In this paper we introduce LingoTowns, a new gwap platform targeting language learners. LingoTowns provides a unified experience integrating games for multiple aspects of lexical and grammatical experience in a single virtual world, whilst simultaneously collecting judgements. Both LingoTowns and its constituent games are designed to provide more engagement to the players/learners than normal gwaps.
