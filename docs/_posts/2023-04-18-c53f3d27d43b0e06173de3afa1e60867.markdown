Due to openness and transparency initiatives, a vast amount of data is being made publicly available. This data has great significance for business and society. However, it also led to challenges that need to be overcome for this data to reach its full potential. In this paper, we are focusing on the problem of connecting open data portals (ODPs) through metadata alignment. We investigate the available metadata accompanying datasets, especially the part related to categories datasets belong to  Cites: Linguistic regularities in continuous space word representations
