Most of the news headline generation models that use the sequence-to-sequence model or recurrent network have two shortcomings: the lack of parallel ability of the model and easily repeated generation of words. It is difficult to select the important words in news and reproduce these expressions, resulting in the headline that inaccurately summarizes the news.
