In order to analyze sentiment data of foreign literary works, this paper proposes an algorithm for sentiment classification of literary works. We do this by fusing different features of literary works, which in turn captures more feature information for the classifier. As traditional word embedding models are difficult to achieve fusion with the sentiment features of literary works, we consider a multifeature fusion approach of word embedding features and lexical features of literary works.
