Transformer based machine learning called Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers (BERT) is one the recent technique developed by Google designed to help computers understand the meaning of ambiguous language in text by using surrounding text to establish context. In our research, we will be leveraging this model to understand emotions from textual data.
