The OpenAI Generative Pre-trained Transformer ChatGPT has sparked a firestorm of commentary. Responses are both both favorable and unfavorable, often revolving around ethics. Generative AI, based on trained Large Language Models (LLM) definitely have some underlying ethical issues. As they are likely here to stay however, ethics are best exercised in how these tools are applied and their data are verified.
