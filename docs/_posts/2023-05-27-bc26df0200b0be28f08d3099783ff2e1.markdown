Most existing stylistic text rewriting methods operate on a sentence level, but ignoring the broader context of the text can lead to generic, ambiguous, and incoherent rewrites. In this paper, we propose the integration of preceding textual context into both the rewriting and evaluation stages of stylistic text rewriting, focusing on formality, toxicity, and sentiment transfer tasks.
