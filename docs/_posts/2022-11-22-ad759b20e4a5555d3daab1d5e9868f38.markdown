The social recommender system can accurately recommend information to users, according to their interests based on the characteristics of their social network, however, the interaction between users has not been fully captured in the existing social recommender systems. This study contributes to the literature by proposing a social recommendation method on the basis of opinion dynamics, which captures the information on the interactions between target users and opinion leaders.
