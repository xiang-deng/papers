In recent years, many troll accounts have emerged to manipulate social media opinion. Detecting and eradicating trolling is a critical issue for social-networking platforms because businesses, abusers, and nation-state-sponsored troll farms use false and automated accounts. NLP techniques are used to extract data from social networking text, such as Twitter tweets.
