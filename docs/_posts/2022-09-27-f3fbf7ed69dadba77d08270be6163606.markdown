Sarcasm is generally characterized as ironic or satirical that is intended to blame, mock, or amuse in an implied way. Recently, pre-trained language models, such as BERT, have achieved remarkable success in sarcasm detection. However, there are many problems that cannot be solved by using such state-of-the-art models. One problem is attribute information of entities in sentences.
