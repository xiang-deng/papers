Foundation models (FMs) are a new paradigm in AI. First pretrained on broad data at immense scale and subsequently adapted to more specific tasks, they achieve high performances and unlock powerful new capabilities to be leveraged in many domains, including healthcare. This SIG will bring together researchers and practitioners within the CHI community interested in such emerging technology and healthcare.
