In this work we describe our submission to the product ranking task of the Amazon KDD Cup 2022. We rely on a receipt that showed to be effective in previous competitions: we focus our efforts towards efficiently training and deploying large language odels, such as mT5, while reducing to a minimum the number of task-specific adaptations. Despite the simplicity of our approach, our best model was less than 0.004 nDCG@ 20 below the top submission.
