Misinformation could potentially have severe consequences for society, ranging from healthcare to politics. In order to address the negative impact of misinformation, there is a need for tools and technologies that can automatically identify misinformation. Towards this end, we examine the effectiveness of seven different linguistic cues with respect to three datasets.
