There is a need for an automated approach to extract current trends and perceptions from literature review material in a field of interest. Manually reviewing a large number of papers is time-consuming, topic modelling will help to avoid this. The text mining technique chosen for this task is topic modelling. The chapter gives an overview of the most widely used topic modelling techniques, as well as a few applications.
