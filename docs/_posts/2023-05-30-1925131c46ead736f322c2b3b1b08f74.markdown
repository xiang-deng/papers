The past few decades have seen the rapid development of topic modeling. So far, research has been more concerned with determining the ideal number of topics or meaningful topic clustering words than with applying topic modeling techniques to evaluate linguistic theories. This study proposes the Structural Topic Model (STM)-led framework to facilitate the interpretation of topic modeling results and standardize text analysis.
