The ability of artificial intelligence (AI) to take on complex tasks can facilitate humans life, but also raise concerns about AI replacing the human workforce. Acceptance and prior trust are prerequisites for successfully using AI. Yet, there is no overview of the research streams that covers the topic of AI trust. We, therefore, analyzed all AI trust literature to this date.
