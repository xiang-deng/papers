The categories of venues are labels, such as Museum and Bar, assigned to geographic locations. They are often incomplete and new venues may constantly arise, making automatic venue labeling essential. In this study, we proposed a category-aware venue embedding model with similarity constraints (CAVE-SC), which is an automatic feature engineering method to infer category labels for venues.
