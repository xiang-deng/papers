Mis-and disinformation are now a substantial global threat to our security and safety. To cope with the scale of online misinformation, one viable solution is to automate the fact-checking of claims by retrieving and verifying against relevant evidence. While major recent advances have been achieved in pushing forward the automatic fact-verification, a comprehensive evaluation of the possible attack vectors against such systems is still lacking.
