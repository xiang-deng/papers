Abstract We introduce Landmark Explanation, a framework that extends the capabilities of a post-hoc perturbationbased explainer to the EM scenario. Landmark Explanation leverages on the specific schema typically adopted by the EM datasets, representing pairs of entity descriptions, for generating word-based explanations that effectively describe the matching model.
