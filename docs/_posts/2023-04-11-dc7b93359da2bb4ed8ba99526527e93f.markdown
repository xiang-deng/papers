Generically defined, recommender systems can be characterized as software solutions that provide users convenient access to relevant content. The types of conveniences that such systems provide can be manifold. Historically, recommender systems were mainly designed as information filtering tools, like the early GroupLens system (Resnick et al., 1994) from 1994.
