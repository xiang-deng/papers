Scientific paper summarization aims at generating a short and concise digest while preserving important information of the original document. Currently, scientific paper summarization faces two main challenges. First, inter-sentence relations are hard to learn, especially in the case of long-form scientific papers. Second, structural information of the well-structured scientific papers has not been fully exploited.
