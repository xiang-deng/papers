There is a huge amount of textual information in digital form growing exponentially over the years. With this data explosion, retrieving relevant information through information retrieval (IR) systems has become crucial. Due to the growing popularity of various tools such as voice assistants and chatbots, that rely on humanmachine interaction, it is important that such systems are able to answer any query directly, rather than leading the user to a set of references.
