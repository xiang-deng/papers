Creativity is an inherently human skill, and thus one of the goals of Artificial Intelligence. Specifically, linguistic computational creativity deals with the autonomous generation of linguistically-creative artefacts. Here, we present four types of text that can be tackled in this scope—poetry, humour, riddles, and headlines—and overview computational systems developed for their generation in Portuguese.
