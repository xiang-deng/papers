Phone review is important in car loan audits, in which auditors contact applicants to make risk assessments by how applicants act to a sequence of questions. Due to the length of dialogues, auditors tend to miss important details, thus requiring an aiding system to record the dialogues in a compact form. Existing methods that utilize slot-value pairs to track the latest dialogue states fail to record the intermediate process which is critical for risk assessment.
