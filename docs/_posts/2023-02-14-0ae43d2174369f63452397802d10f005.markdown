Quotes are critical for establishing credibility in news articles. A direct quote enclosed in quotation marks has a strong visual appeal and is a sign of a reliable citation. Unfortunately, this journalistic practice is not strictly followed, and a quote in the headline is often  contextomized.  Such a quote uses words out of context in a way that alters the speaker s intention so that there is no semantically matching quote in the body text.
