Speaker estimation in a manga is one of the components that needs to be recognized in conducting research using manga. To identify the speaker of a text line in a manga, a dataset of who speaks the lines is needed. In order to construct such a dataset easily, we proposed a method to annotate who speaks a text line based on characteristics of information design and the human factor.
