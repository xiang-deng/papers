The huge supporting training data on the Internet has been a key factor in the success of deep learning models. However, it also raises concerns about the unauthorized exploitation of the dataset, eg, for commercial propose, which is forbidden by the dataset licenses. In this paper, we introduce a backdoor-based watermarking approach that can be used as a general framework to protect public-available data.
