Dialogue systems based on neural networks trained on large-scale corpora have a variety of practical applications today. However, using uncensored training corpora may have risks, such as potential social bias issues. Meanwhile, manually reviewing these training corpora for social bias content is costly. So, it is necessary to design a recognition model that automatically detects social bias in dialogue systems.
