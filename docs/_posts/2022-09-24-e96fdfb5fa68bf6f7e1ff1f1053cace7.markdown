People often take user ratings and reviews into consideration when shopping for products or services online. However, such user-generated data contains self-selection bias that could affect people decisions and it is hard to resolve this issue completely by algorithms. In this work, we propose to raise the awareness of the self-selection bias by making three types of information concerning user ratings and reviews transparent.
