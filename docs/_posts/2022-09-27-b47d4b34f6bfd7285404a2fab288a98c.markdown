Dialogue is a process of information exchanging, where global background is stable while local focuses are transiting. Thus, at the ongoing dialogue turn, there are both relevant and irrelevant semantics existing in dialogue contexts. How to filter out noises and selectively utilize context can pave the way to successful dialogue generation.
