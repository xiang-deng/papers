Integrated writing assessments are tasks that require students to construct an essay using evidence from source texts. Topic models are statistical models used to analyze text. This study analyzes two integrated writing tasks from students in grades 6 (N= 1,250) and 8 (N= 1,250) using topic models. Independent evaluations of the essays were also conducted to help generalize the results across the two grades and better understand students underlying writing techniques.
