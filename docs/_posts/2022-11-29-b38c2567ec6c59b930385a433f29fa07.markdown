Although AI is spread across all domains and many authors stated that providing explanations is crucial, another question comes into play: How accurate are those explanations? This paper aims to summarize a state-of-the-art review in XAI evaluation metrics, to present a categorization of evaluation methods and show a mapping between existing tools and theoretically defined metrics by underlining the challenges and future development.
