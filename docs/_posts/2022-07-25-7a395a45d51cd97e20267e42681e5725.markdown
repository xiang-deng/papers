In two studies, we evaluated the trust and usefulness of automated compared to manual parking using an experimental paradigm and by surveying owners of vehicles with automated parking features. In Study 1, we compared participants ability to manually park a Tesla Model X and use the Autopark feature to complete perpendicular and parallel parking maneuvers.
