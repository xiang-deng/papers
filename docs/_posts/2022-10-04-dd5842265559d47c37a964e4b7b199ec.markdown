Location-based social networks (LBSNs) have impacted our lives recently to a great deal. Typical location-based social networking sites have provision of declaring a check-in at a venue for users. Users can communicate this information with their friends, thus, generating huge dataset. This large geographical information has paved the way to build location-based recommender systems.
