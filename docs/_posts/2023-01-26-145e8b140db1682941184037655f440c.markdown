We investigate the data collected for the Accuracy Evaluation Shared Task as a retrospective reproduction study. The shared task was based upon errors found by human annotation of com-puter generated summaries of basketball games. Annotation was performed in three separate stages, with texts taken from the same three systems and checked for errors by the same three annotators.
