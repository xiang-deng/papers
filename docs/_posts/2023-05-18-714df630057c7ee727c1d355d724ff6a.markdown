Even though peer review is a central aspect of scientific communication, research shows that the process reveals a power imbalance. The position of the reviewer allows them to be harsh and intentionally offensive without being held accountable. It casts doubt on the integrity of the peer-review process and transforms it into an unpleasant and traumatic experience for authors. Accordingly, more effort should be given to provide critical and constructive feedback. Hence, it is necessary to remedy Cites: Can we automate scientific reviewing?
