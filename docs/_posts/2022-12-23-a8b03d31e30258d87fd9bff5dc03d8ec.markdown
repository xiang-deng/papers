The widely studied task of Natural Language Inference (NLI) requires a system to recognize whether one piece of text is textually entailed by another, ie whether the entirety of its meaning can be inferred from the other. In current NLI datasets and models, textual entailment relations are typically defined on the sentence-or paragraph-level.
