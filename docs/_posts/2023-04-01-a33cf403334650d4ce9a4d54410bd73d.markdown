Managing emergency events, such as natural disasters, necessitates real-time situational awareness for management teams. This paper presents a novel approach to obtaining accurate and comprehensive summaries of these events by utilizing a state-of-the-art open-source search engine and a large language model to retrieve and summarize information from social media and online news sources.
