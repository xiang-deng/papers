Abstract Evidences in Legal Question Answering (LQA) help infer accurate answers. Current sentence-level evidence extraction based methods may lose the discourse coherence of legal articles since they tend to make the extracted sentences scattered over an article. To this end, this paper proposes a cascaded key segment learning enhanced framework for L ong L egal article Q uestion A nswering, namely L 2 QA.
