In this paper, we propose a method to generate a personalized summary that may be of interest to each user based on the discourse structure of documents in order to deliver a certain amount of coherent and interesting information within a limited time, primarily via a spoken dialog form. We initially constructed a news article corpus with annotations of the discourse structure, users profiles, and interests in sentences and topics.
