Question Difficulty Estimation from Text (QDET) is the application of Natural Language Processing techniques to the estimation of a value, either numerical or categorical, which represents the difficulty of questions in educational settings. We give an introduction to the field, build a taxonomy based on question characteristics, and present the various approaches that have been proposed in recent years, outlining opportunities for further research.
