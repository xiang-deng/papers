Social recommendation leverages social information to alleviate data sparsity and cold-start issues of collaborative filtering (CF) methods. Most existing works model user interests following the assumption of social homophily based on social-relation data. The explicit modeling of social influence, which also largely affects user behaviors, has not been well explored.
