We introduce HunSum-1: a dataset for Hungarian abstractive summarization, consisting of 1.14 M news articles. The dataset is built by collecting, cleaning and deduplicating data from 9 major Hungarian news sites through CommonCrawl. Using this dataset, we build abstractive summarizer models based on huBERT and mT5.
