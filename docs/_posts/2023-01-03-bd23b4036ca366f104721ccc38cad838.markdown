Emoji are often used in social media to enrich users emotions, and they play an important role in the task of social media sentiment analysis. In practice, researchers are more likely to consider emoji as special symbols and treat them separately from the text. Some existing methods use emoji as a dictionary for matching or converting emoji into text.
