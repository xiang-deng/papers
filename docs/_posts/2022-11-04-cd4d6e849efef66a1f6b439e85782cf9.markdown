In this paper, UX design and evaluation metrics for a conversational bot, especially, a voice-bot supporting multi-turn and multi-domain conversations, are studied. For a voice-bot, user satisfaction can be affected by the number of dialogue turns, the number of conversation topics, and the amount of information. New evaluation metrics for UX on the voice-bot are introduced under a persona design framework for the public sector.
