The main goal of the third year of the SnakeCLEF challenge was to provide an evaluation platform that helps track the performance of AI-driven methods for snake species recognition systems on a global scale and allows direct comparison with human experts. We ran two challenges separately for humansexperts and novicesand AI methods in order to lay the groundwork for future comparison between human and machine-based snake species identification.
