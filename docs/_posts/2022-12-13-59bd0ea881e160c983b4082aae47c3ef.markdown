Fund managers ability to evaluate risk has important implications for their portfolio management and performance. We use a state-of-the-art deep learning model to measure fund managers forward-looking risk assessments from their narrative discussions. We validate that managers negative (positive) risk assessments lead to subsequent decreases (increases) in their portfolio risk-taking.
