Existing benchmarks for grounding language in interactive environments either lack real-world linguistic elements, or prove difficult to scale up due to substantial human involvement in the collection of data or feedback signals. To bridge this gap, we develop WebShopa simulated e-commerce website environment with 1.18 million real-world products and 12, 087 crowd-sourced text instructions.
