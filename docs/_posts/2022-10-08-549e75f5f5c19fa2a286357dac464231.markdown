Topic modeling approaches extract the most relevant sets of words (grouped into so-called topics) from a document collection. The extracted topics can be used for analyzing the latent semantic structure hiding in the collection. This task is intrinsically unsupervised (without information about the labels), so evaluating the quality of the discovered topics is challenging.
