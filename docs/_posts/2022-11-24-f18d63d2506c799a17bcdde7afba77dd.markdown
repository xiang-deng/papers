Abstract Information extraction (IE) is a vital step of digitization that reduces paperwork in offices. However, the adaptation of common IE systems to actual business cases faces two issues. First, the number of training samples is small (ie 100200 examples). Second, span extraction models based on question answering formulation require a long time for training and inference. To overcome these issues, we introduce a new query-based model for the extraction of information from  Cites: Natural questions: a benchmark for question answering research
