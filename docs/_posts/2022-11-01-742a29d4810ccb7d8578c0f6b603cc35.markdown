Despite widespread use of LLMs as conversational agents, evaluations of performance fail to capture a crucial aspect of communication: interpreting language in context. Humans interpret language using beliefs and prior knowledge about the world. For example, we intuitively understand the response I wore gloves to the question Did you leave fingerprints? as meaning No .
