Dialogue state tracking (DST) is often used to track the system s understanding of the user goal in task-oriented dialogue systems. Existing DST methods mainly fall into two categories according to their adopted model structure: non-hierarchical and hierarchical models. The former takes the whole dialogue history as inputs during each conversation round, while the latter leverages both an utterance encoder and a dialogue encoder to efficiently model the long-term dialogue dependency.
