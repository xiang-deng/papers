We propose a conceptual perspective on prompts for Large Language Models (LLMs) that distinguishes between (1) diegetic prompts (part of the narrative, eg Once upon a time, I saw a fox... ), and (2) non-diegetic prompts (external, eg Write about the adventures of the fox. ).
