Sentence-by-sentence information extraction from long documents is an exhausting and error-prone task. As the indicator of document skeleton, catalogs naturally chunk documents into segments and provide informative cascade semantics, which can help to reduce the search space. Despite their usefulness, catalogs are hard to be extracted without the assist from external knowledge.
