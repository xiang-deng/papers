Product matching is a fundamental step for the global understanding of consumer behavior in e-commerce. In practice, product matching refers to the task of deciding if two product offers from different data sources (eg retailers) represent the same product. Standard pipelines use a previous stage called blocking, where for a given product offer a set of potential matching candidates are retrieved based on similar characteristics (eg same brand, category, flavor, etc.).
