The idea of equal opportunity enjoys wide acceptance because of the freedom opportunities provide us to shape our lives. Many disagree deeply, however, about the meaning of equal opportunity, especially in algorithmic decision-making. A new theory of equal opportunity adopts a structural approach, describing how decisions can operate as bottlenecks or narrow places in the structure of opportunities.
