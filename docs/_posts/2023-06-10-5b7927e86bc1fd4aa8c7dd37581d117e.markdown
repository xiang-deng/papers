One of the common use cases for data discovery is to enrich a given table with additional columns from related tables inside a data lake. We have recently introduced MATE and COCOA, two systems for joinability discovery and correlation calculation, respectively. By leveraging two novel index structures, a hash-based Super Key Index, and an Order Index, our system is capable of efficiently identifying tables that join on multiple columns and contain relevant features.
