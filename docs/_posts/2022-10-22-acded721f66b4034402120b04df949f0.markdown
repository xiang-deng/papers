Story types depict the development of movie stories in terms of the protagonist s character traits and the motivations that drive him in facing his challenges. We define a novel task of story type classification of movies and propose a lightweight machine learning solution. A crowdsourcing experiment was performed to label 45 movies for their perceived story types.
