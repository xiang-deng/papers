In this paper we propose the task of list verbalisation within a Knowledge Graph Question Answering system. Inspired by the Gricean Maxims of Quantity, Relation, and Manner we show a proof of concept ranking answer candidates through graph-based and language model-based measurements for on the one hand popularity and on the other hand a more pragmatically informed context.
