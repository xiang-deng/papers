Advances in voice technology and voice user interfaces (VUIs)such as Alexa, Siri, and Google Homehave opened up the potential for many new types of interaction. However, despite the potential of these devices reflected by the growing market size and body of VUI research, there is a lingering sense that the technology is still underused. In this paper, we conducted a systematic literature review of 35 papers to identify and synthesize 123 VUI design guidelines into five themes.
