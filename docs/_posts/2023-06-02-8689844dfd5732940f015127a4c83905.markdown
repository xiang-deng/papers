Digital documents are generated, disseminated, and disclosed in books, research papers, newspapers, online feedback, and other content containing large amounts of information, for which discovering topics becomes important but challenging. In the current field of topic modeling, there are limited techniques available to deal with (1) the predominance of frequently occurring words in the estimated topics and (2) the overlap of commonly used words in different topics.
