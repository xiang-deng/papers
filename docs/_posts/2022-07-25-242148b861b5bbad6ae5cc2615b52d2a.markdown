Abstract Paul, Amala Mary Salim, A. Text summarization is the process of generating a condensed text form from single or multiple text documents. Extractive summaries are generated by stringing together selected sentences taken from the input text document without any modification, whereas abstractive summaries convey the salient ideas in the input, may reuse phrases or clauses from the input text, and may add new phrases/sentences which are not part of the input text.
