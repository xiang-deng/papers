Abstract Aspect Sentiment Triplet Extraction (ASTE) aims to extract aspect terms, opinion terms, and the corresponding sentiments from the target sentence, which is a universal subtask in the field of aspect-based sentiment analysis. Recent models achieve considerable performance in a joint learning manner but still suffer from significant limitations, such as the inability to capture span representations accurately and redundant triples are extracted due to ignoring syntactic dependencies.
