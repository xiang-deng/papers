Hispanic-serving institutions (HSIs) graduate 1/3 of all Latinx engineers, yet research focuses on predominantly white institutions. Using survey data from 1901 mechanical engineering undergraduates from 7 geographically diverse US HSIs, we investigate Latinx students career plans using cluster analysis.
