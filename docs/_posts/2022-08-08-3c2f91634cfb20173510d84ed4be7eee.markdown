People are easily duped by fake news and start to share it on their networks. With high frequency, fake news causes panic and forces people to engage in unethical behavior such as strikes, roadblocks, and similar actions. Thus, counterfeit news detection is highly needed to secure people from misinformation threats on social platforms. Filtering fake news manually from social media platforms is nearly impossible, as such an act raises security and privacy concerns for users.
