Where do prescient ideasthose that initially challenge conventional assumptions but later achieve widespread acceptancecome from? Although their outcomes in the form of technical innovation are readily observed, the underlying ideas that eventually change the world are often obscured. Here we develop a novel method that uses deep learning to unearth the markers of prescient ideas from the language used by individuals and groups.
