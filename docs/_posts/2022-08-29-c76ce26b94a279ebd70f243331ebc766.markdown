Analyzing fish school feeding behavior can assist aquaculturists in making successful feeding decisions, which is critical for enhancing farming efficiency and supporting healthy fish growth. Due to the fierce gathering, jumping, chasing and other actions that occur during fish school feeding, images contain varying degrees of noise and significant overlap between fish targets.
