Detecting contractual risk information from construction specifications is crucial to succeeding in construction projects. This paper describes clause classification using the Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers (BERT) method in natural language processing. Seven risk categories are determined from a literature review, including payment, temporal, procedure, safety, role and responsibility, definition, and reference.
