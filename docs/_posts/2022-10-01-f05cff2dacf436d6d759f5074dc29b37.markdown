This thesis aims to examine the use of financial sentiment analysis for quarterly reports published by companies listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange (OSE). Additionally, the intention of the study is to use methods from computer science to enable the transformation of financial reports, from the raw PDF format to the financial sentiment scores.
