Machine learning models are everywhere now; but only few of them are transparent in how they work. To remedy this, local explanations aim to show users how and why learned models produce a certain output for a given input (data sample). However, most existing approaches are oriented around images or text data and, thus, cannot leverage the structure and properties of tabular data.
