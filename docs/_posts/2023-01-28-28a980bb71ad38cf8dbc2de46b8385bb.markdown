Automated claim checking is the task of determining the veracity of a claim given evidence retrieved from a textual knowledge base of trustworthy facts. While previous work has taken the knowledge base as given and optimized the claim-checking pipeline, we take the opposite approachtaking the pipeline as given, we explore the choice of the knowledge base.
