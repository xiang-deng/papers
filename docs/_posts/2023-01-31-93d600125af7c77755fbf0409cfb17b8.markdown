A recent trend among major organisations is to release their datasets in the cloud over various Database-as-a-Service (DBaaS) providers premises, creating a use case for multi-cloud querying. As identified in the literature, middlewares with such capabilities should quote the monetary cost and the response time of the queries in order to gain the trust of their users, and also optimise the queries so as to avoid cost overruns and meet the quotations.
