Semantic similarity is ever evolving. It has roots in similarity and dissimilarity measures used in data science and has grown into four major categories of employable methodologies. There is no single correct answer for employing any of the similarity methods. Employment should be based on the corpora at hand coupled with the desired outcome the researcher is looking for. This paper presents the various methods, broken down by category, along with associated research papers Cites: Word n-gram attention models for sentence similarity and inference
