Recommender systems typically retrieve items from an item corpus for personalized recommendations. However, such a retrieval-based recommender paradigm faces two limitations: 1) the human-generated items in the corpus might fail to satisfy the users diverse information needs, and 2) users usually adjust the recommendations via passive and inefficient feedback such as clicks.
