We present HPD: Harry Potter Dialogue Dataset to facilitate the study of building dialogue agents for characters in a story. It differs from existing dialogue datasets in two aspects: 1) HPD provides rich background information about the novel Harry Potter, including scene, character attributes, and character relations; 2) All these background information will change as the story goes on.
