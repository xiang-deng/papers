Question Generation (QG) is the task of utilizing Artificial Intelligence (Al) technology to generate questions that can be answered by a span of text within a given passage. Existing research on QG in the educational field struggles with two challenges: the mainstream QG models based on seq-to-seq fail to utilize the structured information from the passage; the other is the lack of specialized educational QG datasets.
