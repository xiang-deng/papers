Distantly supervised relation extraction aims to obtain relational facts from unstructured texts. Although distant supervision can automatically generate labeled training instances, it inevitably suffers from the wrong-label problem. Most of the current work is based on the bag-level for solving the noise problem, where a bag is composed of multiple sentences containing mentions of the same entity pair. However, previous studies mostly represent sentences from a single perspective Cites: Modeling relations and their mentions without labeled text
