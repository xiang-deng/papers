In an ever-changing environment, Software as a Service (SaaS) can rarely protect users privacy. Being able to manage and control the privacy is therefore an important goal for SaaS. Once the participant of composite service is substituted, it is unclear whether the composite service satisfy user privacy requirement or not. In this paper, we propose a privacy policies automatic update method to enhance user privacy when a service participant change in the composite service.
