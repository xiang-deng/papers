Various studies have assessed the GHG footprint of the ICT sector (ICT end-user devices, data centers, telecommunication networks) and the potential of ICT use cases (eg smart homes, ride sharing) to avoid GHG emissions in other sectors (eg buildings, transport). We systematically compare relevant studies from the last ten years and discuss the robustness of results in view of the methods used.
