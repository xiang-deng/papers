When applying the popular deep learning based NL2SQL models directly in a specific scenario, problems arise due to the characteristics rooted in background knowledge. In our case, the terminologies and abbreviations in the high value payment system database are the main obstacles. In this paper, a framework that is compatible with BERT-CN and RAT-SQL is proposed for data inquiry tasks within the high value payment system, in which both BERT and RAT-SQL are state of the art Cites: Rat-sql: Relation-aware schema encoding and linking for text-to
