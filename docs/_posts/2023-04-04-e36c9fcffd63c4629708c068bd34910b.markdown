Job-resume matching (JRM) is the core of online recruitment services for predicting the matching degree between a job post and a resume. Most of the existing methods for JRM achieve a promising performance by simplifying this task as a matching between the free-text attributes in the job post and the resume. However, they neglect the contributions of the semistructured multivariate attributes such as education and salary, which will result in an unsuccessful prediction.
