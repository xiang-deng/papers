There is an abundance of data, but a large volume of it is unusable. Data may be noisy, unstructured, stored in incompatible for direct analysis medium or format, and often expensive to access. In most practical cases, the data needs to be processed before it can be used to extract valuable business insights. We refer to the nontrivial, end-to-end operation of extracting intelligence from raw data as an ETL process.
