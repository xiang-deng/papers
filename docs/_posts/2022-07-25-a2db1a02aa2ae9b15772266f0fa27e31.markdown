The popularity of online fashion shopping continues to grow. The ability to offer an effective recommendation to customers is becoming increasingly important. In this work, we focus on Fashion Outfits Challenge, part of SIGIR 2022 Workshop on eCommerce. The challenge is centered around Fill in the Blank (FITB) task that implies predicting the missing outfit, given an incomplete outfit and a list of candidates.
