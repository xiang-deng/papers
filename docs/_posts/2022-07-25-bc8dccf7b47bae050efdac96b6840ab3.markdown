In the paper, the problem of classification of texts in the Polish language based on graph document model is discussed. A set of agricultural scientific texts in the Polish language are normalized and converted into directed graphs with words as vertices. Paths in the graph are assigned weights depending on how often words appear next to each other. The phrases with the highest weight are selected to represent the particular text in every graph.
