Noun compound interpretation is the task of expressing a noun compound (eg chocolate bunny) in a free-text paraphrase that makes the relationship between the constituent nouns explicit (eg bunny-shaped chocolate). We propose modifications to the data and evaluation setup of the standard task (Hendrickx et al., 2013), and show that GPT-3 solves it almost perfectly.
