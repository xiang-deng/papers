Fact verification has attracted a lot of research attention recently, eg, in journalism, marketing, and policymaking, as misinformation and disinformation online can sway one s opinion and affect one s actions. While fact-checking is a hard task in general, in many cases, false statements can be easily debunked based on analytics over tables with reliable information. Hence, table-based fact verification has recently emerged as an important and growing research area.
