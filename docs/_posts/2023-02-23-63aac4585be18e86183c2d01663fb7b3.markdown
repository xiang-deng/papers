Modern news aggregators do the hard work of organizing a large news stream, creating collections for a given news story with tens of source options. This paper shows that navigating large source collections for a news story can be challenging without further guidance. In this work, we design three interfaces--the Annotated Article, the Recomposed Article, and the Question Grid--aimed at accompanying news readers in discovering coverage diversity while they read.
