Selecting relevant data subsets from large, unfamiliar datasets can be difficult. We address this challenge by modeling and visualizing two kinds of auxiliary information:(1) quality-the validity and appropriateness of data required to perform certain analytical tasks; and (2) usage-the historical utilization characteristics of data across multiple users.
