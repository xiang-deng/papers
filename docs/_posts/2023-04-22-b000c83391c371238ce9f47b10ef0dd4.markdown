Text Summarization is the process of extracting essential parts from a source text. The generated summary will aid in decreasing the time and effort necessary to read the complete text. We will look into the use case of text summarization of meeting transcripts in this paper. We look at how to find words and phrases that effectively reflect or contribute to the semantic essence of decisions made in multi-party human-to-human talks.
