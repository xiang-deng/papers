Periodization has been neglected in computational literary studies despite some early discussions (Underwood 2013). In literary studies the usual basis for the construction of periods are differences in the choice of topics or style or non-literary aspects, while differences in the representation of emotions are underresearched. This is the case even though recent approaches in literary studies ascribe epochspecific relevance to the literary representation of emotions.
