The cold-start problem has been of great concern in the recommendation domain. To address this problem, meta-learning frameworks have been widely adopted due to their fast adaptation to new tasks. However, existing meta-learning-based methods always use higher-order graph structures to obtain global user preferences but neglect to consider local preferences at different rating levels in a fine-grained manner.
