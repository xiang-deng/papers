Systems that annotate tabular data semantically have witnessed increasing attention from the community in recent years; this process is commonly known as Semantic Table Annotation (STA). Its objective is to map individual table elements to their counterparts from a Knowledge Graph (KG). Individual cells and columns are assigned to KG entities and classes to disambiguate their meaning.
