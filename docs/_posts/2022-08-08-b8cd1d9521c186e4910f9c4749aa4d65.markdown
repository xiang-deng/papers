A new method to extract knowledge structured as n-Ary relations from scientific articles is presented. We designed and assessed different approaches to reconstruct instances of n-Ary relations extracted from scientific articles in experimental domains, driven by an Ontological and Terminological Resource (OTR) and based on multi-feature representation of relations and their arguments.
