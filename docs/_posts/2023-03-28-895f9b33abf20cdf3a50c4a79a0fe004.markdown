Abstract Machine Reading Comprehension (MRC) is the task of teaching machines to understand and answer questions based on a given text. In the judicial domain, MRC has garnered attention as a novel strategy for addressing a variety of legal issues. Judicial MRC generally requires interpretability, meaning that the model should not only answer the question correctly but also provide supporting evidence sentences.
