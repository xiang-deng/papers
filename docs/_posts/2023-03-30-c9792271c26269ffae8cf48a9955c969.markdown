According to the literature, customer service failure and negative customer reactions are closely associated. However, there has been little exploration of the relationship between service failure and negative customer reactions in the context of artificial intelligence (AI) services. Based on a conceptual model, this study explores the impact of voice AI service failure on customer complaint behavior.
