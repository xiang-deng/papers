This paper summarizes the second Automated Legal Question Answering Competition (ALQAC 2022). ALQAC aims to tackle the tasks of legal text processing for low-resource languages. This year, we organize two tasks on Vietnamese statute law documents: Legal Document Retrieval (Task 1) and Legal Question Answering (Task 2). Task 1 requires a system to retrieve the related legal articles to a legal question, and Task 2 requires a system to accomplish the answer to a legal question Cites: BoolQ: Exploring the surprising difficulty of natural yes/no questions
