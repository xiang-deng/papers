This thesis focuses on the framing and content analysis of the text articles of CNN before and after the war. The aim was to find out the frames and possible differences among them. One result is that President Zelensky was framed neutrally and not as a hero as we might have expected. President Biden was framed similarly–neutrally and without heroic or similar frames.
