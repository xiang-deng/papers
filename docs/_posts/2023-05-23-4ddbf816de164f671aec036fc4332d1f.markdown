In this brief review, we report on the state of knowledge and its limitations regarding the environmental footprint of the digital sector. While the final energy consumption and the related carbon footprint of information and communication technologies (ICT) are well studied, other environmental factors and some phases of the life cycle are still poorly understood. New connected equipment and services currently being deployed make such assessments even more complex.
