In recent years, group buying has become one popular kind of online shopping activity, thanks to its larger sales and lower unit price. Unfortunately, research seldom focuses on recommendations specifically for group buying by now. Although some recommendation models have been proposed for group recommendation, they can not be directly used to achieve real-world group buying recommendation, due to the essential difference between group recommendation and group buying Cites: Recommender systems with social regularization
