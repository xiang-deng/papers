In AI-assisted decision-making, a central promise of putting a human in the loop is that they should be able to complement the AI system by adhering to its correct and overriding its mistaken recommendations. In practice, however, we often see that humans tend to over-or under-rely on AI recommendations, meaning that they either adhere to wrong or override correct recommendations.
