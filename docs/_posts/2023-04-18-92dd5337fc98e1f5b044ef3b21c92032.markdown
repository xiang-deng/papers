Abstractive multi-document summarization (MDS) paraphrases the salient key information scattered across multiple documents. Due to the large length of the documents, most previous methods opt to first extract salient sentence-level information and then summarize it. However, they neglect the aspect information: documents are often well-organized and written down according to certain aspects.
