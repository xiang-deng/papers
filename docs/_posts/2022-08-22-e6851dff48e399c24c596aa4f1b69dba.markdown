There are many cases where the safety and security of systems are threatened by accidental or intentional human error. This study focuses on the fact that there is information available about human error in design and operation documents and case reports, and they are in natural language. Therefore, we propose a method to analyze the impact of human error on safety and security using Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA), which is one of the topic model methods.
