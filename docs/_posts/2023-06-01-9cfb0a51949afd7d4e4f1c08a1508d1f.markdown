Many enterprise systems are document-intensive and require extensive manual verification. The verification process has challenge in terms of time and remaining bugs. A general automatic or semi-automatic document verification system would be useful. However, as the nature of the natural language, the context is an important factor. In this research, the target context is selected to be the financial documents, which have been highly interested recently.
