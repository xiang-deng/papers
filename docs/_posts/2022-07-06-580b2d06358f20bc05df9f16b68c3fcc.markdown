Story ending generation aims at generating reasonable endings for a given story context. Most existing studies in this area focus on generating coherent or diversified story endings, while they ignore that different characters may lead to different endings for a given story. In this paper, we propose a Character-oriented Story Ending Generator (CoSEG) to customize an ending for each character in a story.
