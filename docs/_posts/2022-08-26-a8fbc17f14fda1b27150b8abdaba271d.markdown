Sentiment analysis aids in obtaining the opinion of the users towards a particular product, service or policy. Focusing on classifying the sentiment that corresponds to each aspect of the entity in the document will help to identify the sentiment more clearly. This is also the mission of aspect-based sentiment analysis (ABSA). The vast majority of prior studies in ABSA have implemented single-task execution models on single-domain datasets.
