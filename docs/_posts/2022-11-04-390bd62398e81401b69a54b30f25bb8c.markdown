In this paper, we introduce the task of modeling interpersonal relationships for story generation. For addressing this task, we propose Relationships as Latent Variables for Story Generation,(ReLiSt). ReLiSt generates stories sentence by sentence and has two major components-a relationship selector and a story continuer.
