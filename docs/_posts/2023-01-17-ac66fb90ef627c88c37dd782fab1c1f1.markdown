In this study, we explored the public conceptualization of creativity on Twitter by analyzing co-listed hashtags associated with the term# creativity in millions of tweets. Exploratory Graph Analysis was used to identify a network of semantic clusters, and a pre-trained language model yielded the sentiment of the underlying tweets.
