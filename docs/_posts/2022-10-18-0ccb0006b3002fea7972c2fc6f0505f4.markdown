In this article, we offer an introduction to the notion of analogical explanations. Because analogical reasoning is a widely used type of reasoning, we take the view that analogy-based explanations will be acceptable for humans. The cornerstone of the approach is the concept of analogical proportion (ie, statements of the form a is to b as c is to d), comparing 2 pairs of items.
