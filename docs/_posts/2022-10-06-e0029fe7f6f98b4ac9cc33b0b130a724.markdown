Business Knowledge Graph is important to many enterprises today, providing the factual knowledge and structured data that steer many products and make them more intelligent. Despite the welcome outcome, building business KG brings prohibitive issues of deficient structure, multiple modalities and unmanageable quality. In this paper, we advance the practical challenges related to building KG in non-trivial real-world systems.
