The topic information of the dialogue text is important for the model to understand the intentions of the dialogue participants and to abstractly summarize the content of the dialogue. The dialogue topic extraction task aims to extract the evolving topic information in long dialogue texts. In this work, we focus on topic extraction of dialogue texts in customer service scenarios.
