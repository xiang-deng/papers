Artificial intelligence (AI) systems can cause harm to people. This research examines how individuals react to such harm through the lens of blame. Building upon research suggesting that people blame AI systems, we investigated how several factors influence people s reactive attitudes towards machines, designers, and users. The results of three studies (N= 1,153) indicate differences in how blame is attributed to these actors.
