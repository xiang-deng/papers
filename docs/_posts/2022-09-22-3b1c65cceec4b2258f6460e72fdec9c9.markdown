It has never been easier to get access to content. Rather, we are facing an ever increasing overload which undermines the ability to identify high-quality content relevant to the user. Automatic summarization techniques have been developed to distil down the content to the key points and shorten therewith the time required to grasp the essence and judge about the relevance of the document.
