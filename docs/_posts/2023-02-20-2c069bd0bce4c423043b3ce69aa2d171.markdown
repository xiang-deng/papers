Neural open-domain dialogue systems often fail to engage humans in long-term interactions on popular topics such as sports, politics, fashion, and entertainment. However, to have more socially engaging conversations, we need to formulate strategies that consider emotion, relevant-facts, and user behaviour in multi-turn conversations.
