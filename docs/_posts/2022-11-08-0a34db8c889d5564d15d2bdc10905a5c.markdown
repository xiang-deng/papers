With the increasing use of social media with its ability for users to share comments immediately, the extent of a system to identify offensive content has become a necessity in all languages. Due to the lack of publicly available resources on offensive language identification for Farsi, which has more than 110 million speakers, we present Pars-OFF, a three-layered annotated corpus for offensive language detection in Farsi to fill the existing gap.
