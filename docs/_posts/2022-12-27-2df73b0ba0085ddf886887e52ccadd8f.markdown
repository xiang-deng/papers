Topic modeling is one of the most popular analyses, which extracts the topics from the text. This paper proposes a method to extract the most-discussed topics for 32 countries of the world. In this regard, more than five million related tweets have been studied, and a method based on content analysis is proposed to identify the exact location of each tweet.
