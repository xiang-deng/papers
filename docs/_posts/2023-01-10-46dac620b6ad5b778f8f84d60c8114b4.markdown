COVID-19 is a new virus that first appeared in the year 2020 and is still currently plaguing our world. With the emergence of this virus, much information, both fake and real, has circulated in the internet. Fake information can lead to misleading information and cause a riot in society. In this paper, we aim to build a hoax detection system using the pre-trained transformer models BERT, RoBERTa, DeBERTa and Electra.
