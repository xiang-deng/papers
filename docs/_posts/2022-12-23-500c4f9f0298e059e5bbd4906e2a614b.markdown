This article aims to review the literature on condition-based maintenance (CBM) by analyzing various terms, applications, and challenges. CBM is a maintenance technique that monitors the existing condition of an industrial asset to determine what maintenance needs to be performed. This article enlightens the readers with research in condition-based maintenance using machine learning and artificial intelligence techniques and related literature.
