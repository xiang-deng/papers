In a variety of sectors, we observe the emerging need for responding to user questions in a fast and efficient manner. We argue that addressing this need by developing question answering reader system applications will lead to several benefits: a) the density of call centers is reduced, and b) time is saved by getting answers through the application instead of going to the company itself.
