As a popular research field of sentiment analysis, aspect-based sentiment analysis focuses on the emotional expression in different aspects. However, the current research is not precise enough in dividing the aspects of sentiment analysis. The problem of semantic overlap between aspects occurs. Furthermore, in many cases, one aspect may be contained in several sub-aspects.
