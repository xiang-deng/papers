Existing benchmarks for open-domain question answering (ODQA) typically focus on questions whose answers can be extracted from a single paragraph. By contrast, many natural questions, such as What players were drafted by the Brooklyn Nets? have a list of answers. Answering such questions requires retrieving and reading from many passages, in a large corpus.
