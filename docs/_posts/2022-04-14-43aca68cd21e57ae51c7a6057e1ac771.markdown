Noun-noun compounds (NNCs) occur frequently in the English language. Accurate NNC interpretation, ie determining the implicit relationship between the constituents of a NNC, is crucial for the advancement of many natural language processing tasks. Until now, computational NNC interpretation has been limited to approaches involving linguistic representations only.
