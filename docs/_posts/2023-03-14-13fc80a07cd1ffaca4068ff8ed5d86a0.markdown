Wikipedia plays a crucial role in the integrity of the Web. This work analyzes the reliability of this global encyclopedia through the lens of its references. We operationalize the notion of reference quality by defining reference need (RN), ie, the percentage of sentences missing a citation, and reference risk (RR), ie, the proportion of non-authoritative references.
