Opinions play a major role in almost every human practice. Finding product and service reviews is made easy online. Product reviews are readily available in huge quantities. Considering each review and making a concise decision about a product is not feasible or even possible. Aspect-based sentiment analysis (ABSA) is one of the best solutions to this problem. Summary and online review s analysis is delivered in this paper. ABSA has made extensive use of machine learning techniques.
