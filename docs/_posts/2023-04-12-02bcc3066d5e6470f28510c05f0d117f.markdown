Learning is a multi-faceted phenomenon of critical importance and hence attracted a great deal of research, both experimental and theoretical. In this review, we will consider some of the paradigmatic examples of learning and discuss the common themes in theoretical learning research, such as levels of modeling and their corresponding relation to experimental observations and mathematical ideas common to different types of learning.
