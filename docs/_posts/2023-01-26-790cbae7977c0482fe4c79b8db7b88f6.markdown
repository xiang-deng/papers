Artificial intelligence (AI) has become part of our everyday lives, and its presence and influence are expected to grow exponentially. Regardless of its expanding impact, the perplexing algorithms and processes that drive AI s decision and output can lead to decreased trust, and thus impede the adoption of future AI services.
