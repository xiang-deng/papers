Advancements in artificial intelligence are rapidly increasing. The newgeneration large language models, such as ChatGPT and GPT4, bear the potential to transform educational approaches, such as peerfeedback. To investigate peerfeedback at the intersection of natural language processing (NLP) and educational research, this paper suggests a crossdisciplinary framework that aims to facilitate the development of NLPbased adaptive measures for supporting peerfeedback  Cites: A Dataset of Information-Seeking Questions and Answers
