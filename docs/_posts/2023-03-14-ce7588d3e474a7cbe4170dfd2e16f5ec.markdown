The abundance of benchmark datasets supports the recent trend of increased attention given to Question Answering (QA) tasks. However, most of them lack a diverse selection of QA types and more challenging questions. In this work, we present StoryQA, a new task and dataset addressing diverse QA problems for both in-context and out-of-context questions.
