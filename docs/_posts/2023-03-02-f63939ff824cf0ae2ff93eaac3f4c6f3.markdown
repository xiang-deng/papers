The Jigsaw is a cooperative learning model that is widely used to organize small group classroom activities so that students have to be dependent on each other to succeed. There are a number of challenges, however, in supporting individual students and groups of students during jigsaw activities. In this paper, we present an AI partner designed to support jigsaw activities in middle school classrooms through dialogue.
