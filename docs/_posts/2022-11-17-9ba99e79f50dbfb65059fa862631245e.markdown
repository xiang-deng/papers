There is a growing frustration amongst researchers and developers in Explainable AI (XAI) around the lack of consensus around what is meant by explainability . Do we need one definition of explainability to rule them all? In this paper, we argue why a singular definition of XAI is neither feasible nor desirable at this stage of XAI s development.
