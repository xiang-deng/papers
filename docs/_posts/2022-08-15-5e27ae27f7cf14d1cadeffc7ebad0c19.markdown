A number of recent studies have investigated online anonymous (dark web) marketplaces. Almost all leverage a measurement-by-proxy design, in which researchers scrape market public pages, and take buyer reviews as a proxy for actual transactions, to gain insights into market size and revenue. Yet, we do not know if and how this method biases results.
