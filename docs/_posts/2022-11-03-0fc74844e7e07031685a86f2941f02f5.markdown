This work introduces a natural language inference (NLI) dataset that focuses on the validity of statements in legal wills. This dataset is unique because:(a) each entailment decision requires three inputs: the statement from the will, the law, and the conditions that hold at the time of the testator s death; and (b) the included texts are longer than the ones in current NLI datasets.
