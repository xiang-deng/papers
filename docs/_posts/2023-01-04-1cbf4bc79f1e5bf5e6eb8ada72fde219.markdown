Person reidentification, ie, retrieving a person of interest across several non-overlapping cameras, is a task that is far from trivial. Despite its great commercial value and wide range of applications (eg, surveillance, intelligent environments, forensics, service robotics, marketing), it remains unsolved, even when the individuals do not change clothes during the recognition period. This paper provides an outlook on long-term person reidentification, an emerging research topic  Cites: Constraint classification for multiclass classification and ranking
