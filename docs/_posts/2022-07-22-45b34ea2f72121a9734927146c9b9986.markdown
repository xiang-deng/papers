The development and diffusion of ontologies allowed the creation of large banks of information regarding multiple domains known as knowledge bases. Ontologies propose a way to represent information providing semantic meaning that allows the data to be machine-interpretable. However, enjoying such rich knowledge is a difficult task for the majority of potential users who do not know either the knowledge-base definition or how to write queries with SPARQL.
