This paper presents a novel task to generate frequently asked question (FAQ) pairs for the rule and regulation documents. It offers an easy way for customers and employers to quickly gain knowledge of them and provides a potential corpus for question-answering robots. While previous work focuses on web texts (eg, Wiki), we generate FAQ pairs from the formal and verbose rule and regulation documents, which is significant in real scenarios.
