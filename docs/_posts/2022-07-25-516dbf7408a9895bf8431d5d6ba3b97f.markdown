Reasoning over conflicting information, ie to argue is at the core of many types of conversation such as persuasion, negotiation and deliberation. The multitude of setups that require this capacity makes it an interesting, yet challenging task for conversational agents. It is required in any scenario in which the corresponding system has to operate on conflicting information or positions, as for example contradicting online reviews or opposing points of view.
