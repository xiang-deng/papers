A pre-trained model based on a large-scale corpus can effectively improve the performance of open-domain dialogue systems in terms of performance. However, recent studies have shown various ethical issues in pre-trained models that seriously affect the application of dialogue systems. Social bias is particularly complex among these ethical issues because its negative impact on marginalized populations is often implicit and therefore requires normative reasoning and rigorous analysis.
