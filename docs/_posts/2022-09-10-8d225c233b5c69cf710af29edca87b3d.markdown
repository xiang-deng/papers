Pre-trained language models have achieved striking success in natural language processing (NLP), leading to a paradigm shift from supervised learning to pre-training followed by fine-tuning. The NLP community has witnessed a surge of research interest in improving pre-trained models. This article presents a comprehensive review of representative work and recent progress in the NLP field and introduces the taxonomy of pre-trained models.
